About Me

Hi, my name is Amisah. I am an aspiring Front-End Developer with a strong interest in web development and design. I am currently focused on building responsive and user-friendly websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I hold a Master’s degree in Education from the International Islamic University Malaysia (IIUM) and have experience as a Customer Service representative and Content Reviewer, which strengthened my communication, attention to detail, and problem-solving skills. I am passionate about creating clean, modern web interfaces and continuously improving my skills to deliver better user experiences.
